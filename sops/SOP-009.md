## 1. Purpose

To ensure all pending and failed bookings in **ELLIS** are identified, reviewed, and resolved correctly. This task guarantees that neither the hotel nor the agent has an active confirmation for a booking that has failed on the system, preventing disputes, double bookings, and unnecessary charges.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all pending / failed bookings visible in ELLIS, checked **twice daily** by the front desk or booking management team. **Special attention is required for bookings processed through CMS channels: Sanha, TL-Lincoln, and Hotelink.**

## 3. Responsibilities

- **Front Desk / Booking Team**: Check ELLIS twice daily for pending/failed bookings, assess error messages, verify with hotel and agent as required, and take the appropriate resolution action.
- **Hotel Operations**: Confirm whether a failed booking was received and process free cancellation if applicable.
- **Finance / Related Team**: Receive and process loss reports for unresolvable cases.

## 4. Procedure

### Step 1 — Daily Check Schedule

This task must be performed **TWICE per day**, every working day:

| Check Session | Recommended Time |
|---|---|
| **Morning Check** | Start of shift / 9:00 AM |
| **Afternoon Check** | End of shift / 5:00 PM |

- Open ELLIS and filter for bookings with status: **Pending** or **Failed / Available**.
- Review each booking individually — **do not bulk-skip** without checking the error message.

### Step 2 — Review Error Message on ELLIS

- For each pending / failed booking, **read the error message carefully** in ELLIS.
- Determine the action required based on the error type — refer to the **Error Action Reference Table** below.
- Errors that can be ignored: confirm no booking was transmitted, log as ignored, and close.
- Errors that require action: proceed to Step 3.

### Step 3 — CMS Channel Bookings: Extra Caution

> ⚠️ **CMS CHANNELS — EXTRA CAUTION REQUIRED**
>
> **Channels: Sanha | TL-Lincoln | Hotelink**
>
> These CMS channels may transmit bookings to the hotel **even when the booking appears failed on the agent side**. **Always contact the hotel directly** to verify whether they received the booking.

For all bookings via **Sanha, TL-Lincoln, or Hotelink** — regardless of the error message — always contact the hotel directly to confirm if they received the booking.

- **Hotel did NOT receive the booking**: Confirm with ELLIS that the booking is fully failed → Log as resolved → No further action.
- **Hotel DID receive the booking**: Proceed to **Step 4**.

### Step 4 — Hotel Received the Failed Booking

Request free cancellation from the hotel, explaining that the booking failed on the agent side.

#### Case A: Hotel Agrees to Free Cancellation

- Process the free cancellation with the hotel.
- Update ELLIS and close the booking. ✔ Done.

#### Case B: Hotel Applies Full Charge (Cancellation Deadline Passed)

- Contact the Agent (AGT) to explain the situation.
- Advise the agent that the booking was confirmed on the VNOP/hotel side.
- **Agent can confirm booking to guest → Guest accepts**: Keep booking as normal. ✔ Done.
- **Agent confirmed the booking was FAILED to the guest**: Update the **Loss Report** → Escalate to supervisor and related team for loss recovery.

## 5. Notes

- This check must be performed **TWICE per day** without exception — morning and afternoon. Skipping a session may result in undetected failed bookings becoming charged bookings.
- Always read the ELLIS error message before deciding to act or ignore — do not assume all failed bookings are the same.
- **CMS channel bookings (Sanha, TL-Lincoln, Hotelink) MUST be verified with the hotel directly** — even if ELLIS shows the booking as failed.
- If the hotel applies a full charge due to a passed cancellation deadline and the agent has already informed the guest of failure, the **loss must be recorded in the Loss Report**.
- Never assume a failed booking is harmless — always verify both the hotel side and agent side are aligned.
- All actions taken (checked, verified, cancelled, loss reported) must be logged in ELLIS immediately.

## Error Message Action Reference Table

| Error Type / Message | Action Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Connection timeout / System error** | Check with hotel & agent | Booking may or may not have reached the hotel — must verify both sides |
| **Duplicate booking error** | Check with hotel & agent | Confirm if one or both bookings were received. Cancel the duplicate accordingly |
| **Rate / availability error** | Can typically ignore | Booking did not go through — confirm with ELLIS that no booking was sent to hotel |
| **CMS channel error** (Sanha, TL-Lincoln, Hotelink) | **Must check with hotel** | High risk — hotel may have received the booking despite failure on agent side |
| **Payment / billing error** | Check with related team | Escalate to finance or relevant team for resolution |
