## 1. Purpose

To ensure all booking reconfirmations are handled accurately by verifying booking information on the ELLIS system against hotel records, updating confirmation numbers when matched, and resolving discrepancies through direct hotel communication.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all confirmed bookings that require reconfirmation, processed by the front desk or booking management team.

## 3. Responsibilities

- **Front Desk / Booking Team**: Retrieve booking details from ELLIS, verify all information fields, update HCN upon match, and contact hotel to resolve any mismatches.
- **Hotel Operations**: Respond to inquiries and adjust booking information when discrepancies are identified.

## 4. Procedure

### Step 1 — Receive Reconfirmation Request

- Receive the reconfirmation request from Agent (AGT).
- Open the booking in the **ELLIS** system.

### Step 2 — Verify Booking Information on ELLIS

Check **all 5 fields** below on ELLIS against the hotel confirmation:

| # | Field | What to Check |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Guest Name** | Full guest name matches exactly between ELLIS and hotel confirmation. |
| 2 | **Check-in Date** | Check-in date is correct and consistent with the original booking. |
| 3 | **Check-out Date** | Check-out date matches the intended stay duration. |
| 4 | **Room Rate** | Room rate matches the agreed rate in the original booking. |
| 5 | **Cancellation Policy** | Cancellation policy terms are correctly reflected in the system. |

### Step 3 — All Information Matched?

#### Case A: **All Information Matched** ✅

- Update the Hotel Confirmation Number (HCN) on ELLIS.
- Inform Agent that the booking has been successfully reconfirmed.
- Close the booking. ✔ Done.

#### Case B: **Information Mismatched** ❌

- Identify the mismatched field(s) (예: wrong guest name, incorrect dates, rate discrepancy).
- Contact the hotel to request correction of the mismatched information.
- **Hotel adjusts the information?**
  - ✅ **YES**: Verify the update → Update HCN on ELLIS → Inform agent → Close the booking. ✔ Done.
  - ❌ **NO / No response**: Escalate to supervisor → Follow up with hotel → Inform agent of the delay.

## 5. Notes

- Always verify **ALL 5 fields** on ELLIS before confirming a match — **do not skip any field**.
- If any single field is mismatched, proceed to Case B and contact the hotel before updating the HCN.
- Document all communications with hotels and agents in the booking system.
- If the hotel does not respond within the expected timeframe, escalate to the supervisor immediately.
- Ensure the agent is informed promptly at every stage to avoid delays in guest preparation.
