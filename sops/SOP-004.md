## 1. Purpose

To ensure all free cancellation requests from agents are evaluated and handled consistently. VNOP will only support and contact the hotel when the guest provides a reasonable reason with related supporting documents. Requests without a reasonable reason will be **auto-rejected without hotel contact**, and the booking will be kept as full charge.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all active bookings for which an agent submits a free cancellation request on behalf of the guest, processed by the front desk or booking management team.

## 3. Responsibilities

- **Front Desk / Booking Team**: Receive the free cancellation request, evaluate the reason and supporting documents, contact the hotel if applicable, and communicate the outcome to the agent.
- **Hotel Operations**: Review and respond to free cancellation requests forwarded by VNOP.

## 4. Procedure

### Step 1 — Receive & Verify Request

- Receive the free cancellation request from Agent (AGT).
- Check booking details in the system (guest name, hotel, check-in date, booking status).
- Check whether the agent has provided a **reason** and **supporting documents**.

### Step 2 — Evaluate the Cancellation Reason

Refer to the **Reason Reference Table** below to determine whether the reason is reasonable.

#### Case A: **Reasonable Reason + Supporting Document Provided** ✅

- 예시: flight cancellation (with proof), natural disaster, death of family member, medical emergency, government travel ban.
- Contact the hotel to request free cancellation on behalf of the agent.
- **Hotel APPROVES** free cancellation → Process free cancellation → Inform Agent → Done. ✔
- **Hotel REFUSES** → Inform Agent the booking cannot be cancelled for free due to hotel policy → Keep as full charge.

#### Case B: **No Reason Provided OR Unreasonable Reason** ❌

- 예시: change of travel plan, found lower rate, personal preference, no reason given.
- **VNOP does NOT contact the hotel.**
- Auto-reject the cancellation request immediately.
- Inform Agent the request has been rejected — booking remains active with full charge.

## 5. Notes

- **Supporting documents are REQUIRED** for reasonable reasons (예: airline cancellation notice, medical certificate, death certificate). Without documents, treat as Case B.
- **VNOP does NOT contact the hotel** for unreasonable reasons under any circumstances — **no exceptions**.
- All decisions (approved or rejected) must be documented in the booking system with reason details.
- Agents must be informed promptly at every stage to avoid disputes or delays.
- If the hotel does not respond within the expected timeframe, escalate to the supervisor immediately.

## Reason Reference Table

| ✅ Reasonable — **Support** | ❌ Unreasonable — **Auto Reject** |
|---|---|
| Flight cancellation by airline (with proof) | Change of travel plan / Changed mind |
| Natural disaster at destination | Found a lower rate elsewhere |
| Death of family member (with proof) | Personal preference, no supporting reason |
| Medical emergency / hospitalization (with proof) | No reason provided |
| Government travel ban or visa denial | Request made after check-in date |
