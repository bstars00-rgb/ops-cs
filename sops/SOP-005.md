## 1. Purpose

To ensure all date amendment requests from agents are evaluated and handled consistently. VNOP will only support and contact the hotel when the guest provides a reasonable reason with relevant supporting documents. Requests without a reasonable reason will be **auto-rejected without hotel contact**, and the booking will be kept as originally booked with full charge.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all active bookings for which an agent submits a date amendment request (check-in and/or check-out date change) on behalf of the guest, processed by the front desk or booking management team.

## 3. Responsibilities

- **Front Desk / Booking Team**: Receive the date amendment request, evaluate the reason and supporting documents, contact the hotel if applicable, and communicate the outcome to the agent.
- **Hotel Operations**: Review and respond to date amendment requests forwarded by VNOP.

## 4. Procedure

### Step 1 — Receive & Verify Request

- Receive the date amendment request from Agent (AGT).
- Check booking details in the system (guest name, hotel, original check-in / check-out dates, booking status).
- Note the **requested new check-in / check-out dates**.
- Check whether the agent has provided a **reason** and **supporting documents**.

### Step 2 — Evaluate the Amendment Reason

Refer to the **Reason Reference Table** below to determine whether the reason is reasonable.

#### Case A: **Reasonable Reason + Supporting Document Provided** ✅

- 예시: flight cancellation/reschedule (with proof), natural disaster, death of family member, medical emergency, government travel ban.
- Contact the hotel to request a free date amendment on behalf of the agent.
- **Hotel APPROVES** → Update new check-in / check-out dates → Inform Agent → Done. ✔
- **Hotel REFUSES** → Inform Agent that booking dates cannot be amended for free due to hotel policy → Keep original dates with full charge.

#### Case B: **No Reason Provided OR Unreasonable Reason** ❌

- 예시: change of travel plan, found a lower rate, personal preference, no reason given.
- **VNOP does NOT contact the hotel.**
- Auto-reject the date amendment request immediately.
- Inform Agent the request has been rejected — booking remains at original dates with full charge.

## 5. Notes

- **Supporting documents are REQUIRED** for all reasonable reasons. Without documents, treat the request as Case B and auto-reject.
- **VNOP does NOT contact the hotel** for unreasonable reasons under any circumstances — **no exceptions**.
- Clearly document the requested new dates, the reason provided, and the decision in the booking system.
- If the hotel approves the amendment, **double-check that both check-in and check-out dates are correctly updated** in the system before informing the agent.
- If the hotel does not respond within the expected timeframe, escalate to the supervisor immediately.
- Agents must be informed promptly at every stage to avoid disputes or delays.

## Reason Reference Table

| ✅ Reasonable — **Support** | ❌ Unreasonable — **Auto Reject** |
|---|---|
| Flight cancellation / rescheduled by airline (with proof) | Change of travel plan / Changed mind |
| Natural disaster at destination | Found a lower rate or better deal elsewhere |
| Death of family member (with proof) | Personal preference, no supporting reason |
| Medical emergency / hospitalization (with proof) | No reason provided |
| Government travel ban or visa denial | Request made after original check-in date |
