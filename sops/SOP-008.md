## 1. Purpose

To ensure all booking rejections are handled consistently, with the correct compensation applied to agents where required. This SOP covers the full rejection process including upgrade/alternative checks, compensation determination, mistake ownership identification, and the **special handling rule for Agoda bookings**.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all bookings that need to be rejected to the agent, processed by the front desk or booking management team.

## 3. Responsibilities

- **Front Desk / Booking Team**: Receive rejection request, check upgrade/alternative options, send rejection email, process cancellation, apply compensation where required, identify mistake ownership, and follow up with hotel if applicable.
- **Hotel Operations**: Respond to inquiries on whether compensation can be covered from their side.
- **Finance / Related Team**: Receive monthly compensation report for tracking and processing.

## 4. Procedure

### Step 1 — Receive Rejection Request & Check Cancellation Deadline

- Receive the booking rejection request.
- Check the booking's cancellation policy and deadline:
  - **Cancellation deadline is FAR away**: Take note and monitor. If guest **self-cancels** within the free cancellation period → **NO compensation** applies to VNOP. If guest does NOT cancel by themselves before the deadline → proceed with rejection and apply compensation to AGT.
  - **Cancellation deadline is NEAR or PASSED** → proceed immediately to Step 2.

### Step 2 — Check Compensation Eligibility

- Review the reason for rejection — refer to the **Compensation Reference Table** below.
- **Exempted reasons** (NO compensation): Natural disaster, Fire or explosion at hotel, Hotel bankruptcy / permanent closure.
- All **other rejection reasons** → **1 night compensation** applies to AGT.

### Step 3 — Check Upgrade & Alternative Options

Before sending the rejection, check with the hotel:

- **Free room upgrade available?** If YES: offer to agent/guest — if accepted, **no rejection needed**.
- **Alternative hotel available?** If YES: proceed to **Step 3A**. If NO upgrade and NO alternative: proceed to **Step 3B**.

#### Step 3A — Alternative Hotel Available

- Present the alternative hotel option to the agent / guest.
- **Guest accepts**: Create new booking at alternative hotel → Cancel original (free of charge) → Apply 1st night compensation → Close. ✔
- **Guest refuses**: Proceed to Step 3B.

#### Step 3B — No Alternative / Guest Refuses Alternative

- Send the official **rejection email** to Agent (AGT).
- Cancel the booking in the system (free of charge).
- Apply **1st night compensation** to AGT (if not an exempted reason).
- Proceed to Step 4.

### Step 4 — Identify Mistake Ownership (For Compensation Report)

Identify who is responsible for the mistake:

- **Hotel side**: Contact hotel to request if they cover the compensation → Log response → Include in monthly report under '**Hotel Fault**'.
- **VNOP side**: Log as internal error → Include in monthly report under '**VNOP Fault**'.

Document full compensation details (booking reference, reason, amount, fault party) in the booking system.

### Step 5 — Agoda Bookings: Special Rejection Rule

> ⚠️ **AGODA SPECIAL RULE — IMPORTANT**
>
> If Agoda discovers that the rejection reason is **wrong rate**, the compensation is **NOT 1 night** — it becomes **100 USD per room per night**.
>
> **VNOP RULE**: Always reject Agoda bookings with the reason **"Fully Booked"** — regardless of the actual reason. **Never mention wrong rate** as the rejection reason to Agoda.

## 5. Notes

- Always check the cancellation deadline first — if far away, monitor and only reject if the guest does not self-cancel in time.
- Compensation of **1 night booking rate** applies to ALL rejection cases EXCEPT: natural disaster, fire/explosion at hotel, and hotel bankruptcy.
- Always check for **free upgrade** or **alternative hotel** before proceeding with rejection — this may resolve the case without compensation.
- For every compensation case, the **mistake ownership** (hotel fault vs. VNOP fault) must be identified and recorded for the monthly compensation report.
- If the mistake is from the hotel side, always attempt to **recover the compensation cost** from the hotel.
- For **Agoda bookings, ALWAYS use 'Fully Booked'** as the rejection reason — never disclose wrong rate, as this triggers a 100 USD/room/night penalty.
- All rejection decisions, compensation amounts, and communications must be fully documented in the booking system.

## Compensation Reference Table

| ❌ Compensation **REQUIRED** (1 Night Rate) | ✅ Compensation **EXEMPTED** (No Compensation) |
|---|---|
| Hotel rejects booking (wrong rate, overbooking, etc.) | Natural disaster at hotel destination |
| VNOP internal error / system issue | Fire or explosion at the hotel |
| Any other rejection reason not listed as exempted | Hotel bankruptcy / permanent closure |
